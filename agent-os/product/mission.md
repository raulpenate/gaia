# Product Mission

## Problem

Soil degradation in El Salvador and LATAM goes largely undetected until crop failure or desertification is irreversible. Traditional monitoring relies on abstract cartographic dashboards that are inaccessible to the farmers who need actionable insights most.

## Target Users

* **Farmers** in rural El Salvador and LATAM who need field-level soil health diagnostics on mobile devices with unstable connectivity.
* **Environmental watchdogs** and NGOs monitoring land degradation at scale across agricultural regions.

## Solution

SoilMedic SV treats each agricultural plot as an **individual patient** with a dynamic Soil Medical Record. It cross-validates satellite telemetry (soil moisture at 4 depths via Open-Meteo) with qualitative ground symptoms reported by farmers, then uses an AI agent pipeline acting as an **Expert Agronomist** to diagnose soil health and prescribe immediate mitigation actions — all within a clinical emergency-room UX that makes severity instantly legible.
